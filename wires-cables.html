Comprehensive Product Range

We stock every type of electrical and communication cable required for modern infrastructure:

  • FR (Flame Retardant) Wires: The standard for safe residential wiring. These wires are insulated with a specialized PVC compound that resists fire spread, crucial for home safety.
  • FRLSH (Flame Retardant Low Smoke Halogen) Wires: Essential for multi-story buildings, hospitals, and public spaces. In case of a fire, these wires emit negligible smoke and toxic gases, ensuring visibility during evacuation and preventing suffocation.
  • ZHFR (Zero Halogen Flame Retardant) Wires: The ultimate safety choice. These cables emit absolutely zero toxic halogen fumes even at extreme temperatures, highly recommended for closed environments and IT server rooms.
  • Multicore Flexible Cables: Perfect for industrial machinery, elevators, and heavy-duty appliance connections where continuous movement and high voltage handling are required.
  • Submersible Cables: Specialized 3-core flat cables engineered with high-grade waterproof insulation for reliable water pump operations in deep-well environments and borewells.
  • LAN & Data Cables: High-speed Cat6 and Cat6A cables from top brands to support gigabit internet connectivity, CCTV IP networks, and modern smart home automation systems.
  • Armoured Power Cables: Heavy-duty, mechanical-resistant cables designed for underground routing and primary grid connections.

Understanding Wire Gauge (Sq. mm) for Your Project

Using the incorrect wire gauge can lead to excessive heating, voltage drops, and severe fire hazards. Our technical experts are always available to help you choose the right specifications:

  • 0.75 sq. mm - 1.0 sq. mm: Used for basic lighting circuits, fans, and low-power LED fixtures.
  • 1.5 sq. mm: Standard for 6A plug points, televisions, computers, and general appliance sockets.
  • 2.5 sq. mm: Required for 16A power points, operating geysers, microwaves, and standard 1.5-ton split Air Conditioners.
  • 4.0 sq. mm - 6.0 sq. mm: Heavy-duty applications like 2-ton+ Air Conditioners, industrial heaters, main distribution boards, and heavy motor loads.
