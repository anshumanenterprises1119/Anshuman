Reliable Conduit Piping Solutions in Greater Noida

Before the wires go in and the plaster goes on, the conduit system must be perfect. Anshuman Enterprises supplies the highest grade of PVC conduit pipes that can withstand the pressure of concrete slabs and the test of time. Our pipes are manufactured using premium virgin PVC, ensuring they are flexible enough for bends but strong enough to protect your home's electrical lifeblood.

Whether it's a residential apartment project or an industrial shop-floor setup, our conduit range provides a smooth internal finish for easy wire pulling and superior fire resistance.

Our Comprehensive Range

  • Heavy/Medium/Light Duty Pipes: Available in 19mm, 25mm, and 32mm diameters to suit various load requirements.
  • Flexible Conduits (HMS): Perfect for connecting motor terminals, lighting fixtures, and areas requiring tight bends.
  • Conduit Fittings: High-quality junction boxes, bends, couplers, and fan boxes that ensure a seamless piping network.
  • Metal Conduit Accessories: Galvanized iron (GI) boxes and saddles for industrial surface wiring.
  • Casing-Capping: Modern PVC trunking solutions for open wiring requirements.

Why Quality Conduits Matter?

Cheap pipes often crack under the weight of concrete or during the hot summer months in Greater Noida. A cracked pipe means leaked cement slurry, which can block the path for future wiring—leading to expensive rework. Our **AKG and Precision** pipes are tested for high impact resistance, ensuring your project proceeds without any electrical "surprises" during the wiring phase.
