See who is at your door before you open it. Smart, secure, and convenient video door phone installations for modern living in Greater Noida.

Advanced Home Access Security

A Video Door Phone (VDP) is your first line of defense against unwanted visitors. Anshuman Enterprises offers professional VDP installation services, seamlessly integrating smart doorbell cameras with indoor display monitors and electronic locks. Whether for an apartment, an independent villa, or an office, we provide solutions that enhance your safety and convenience.

Our expert team ensures neat wiring, optimal camera angles, and perfect integration with your existing door hardware, allowing you to answer your door safely from inside or even from your smartphone.

Our VDP Installation Services

  • IP & Analog VDP Systems: Installation of high-definition video doorbells that provide crystal clear audio and video.
  • Multi-Screen Display Setup: Install intercom screens on multiple floors or rooms so you never miss a visitor.
  • Electronic Lock Integration: Connect your VDP to an electric strike or magnetic lock, allowing you to unlock the door with the push of a button on the monitor.
  • Mobile App Configuration: Set up remote access so you can see and speak to visitors via your smartphone, even when you aren't home.
  • Existing System Upgrades: Replace old audio intercoms with modern video systems using existing wiring where possible.

Why Install a Video Door Phone?

The ability to visually verify a visitor without opening the door is a crucial safety feature for families, especially for children and the elderly. With modern IP-based systems, you can also record snapshots or short videos of anyone who presses the doorbell, providing an extra layer of security when you are away.
